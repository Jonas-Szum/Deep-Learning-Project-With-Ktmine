LICENSE AGREEMENT
 
THIS LICENSE AGREEMENT is made as of this October 15, 2014 (“Effective Date”) by and between the UNIVERSITY OF MISSISSIPPI, an educational institution with a principal address at University, Mississippi 38677 (“UM”) and CHROMADEX, a corporation organized and existing under the laws of California with a principal address 10005 Muirlands Blvd Suite G, Irvine, California 92618 (“CHROMADEX”)
 
RECITALS
 
WHEREAS, UM is the owner of certain patent applications and other technology related to a proprietary extract of blue green algae.
 
WHEREAS, CHROMADEX wishes to acquire certain rights and licenses with respect to the proprietary extract of blue green algae in accordance with the terms and conditions hereinafter set forth.
 
NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the premises and mutual covenants contained herein, and intending to be legally bound herby, the parties hereto agree as follows:
