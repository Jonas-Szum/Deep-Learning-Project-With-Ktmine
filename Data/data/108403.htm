This AMENDED AND RESTATED EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT (this “Agreement”) is made and entered into as of this 17th day of March 2015, by and between MedAssets, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the “Company”), and Charles Garner (“Executive”).

WHEREAS, Executive is currently serving as the Chief Financial Officer of the Company;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the promises and mutual covenants contained herein and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are mutually acknowledged, the Company and Executive hereby agree as follows:
